About Me:

From a young age, I’ve been enchanted by the beauty around us—whether it’s the vibrant flowers in nature, colorful clothing, or striking art. I’ve always loved recreating that beauty at home through decor and drawings. My passion for creating harmonious spaces led me to study architecture for several semesters in Innsbruck and Zurich. Ultimately, I returned to my true loves: Interior Design and Floral Design. I earned my certificate in Interior Design from ibW Höhere Fachschule Südostschweiz in Chur and studied floristry under the renowned Bulgarian floral designer Joanna Tomova.

Now based by Lake Zurich, I’m dedicated to crafting spaces that reflect the unique essence of each individual. I believe that while we are all distinct, we share a beautiful connection that inspires my work. With a positive, empathetic, and communicative approach, I strive to develop interiors that are not only visually stunning but also nurturing.

My philosophy is simple: great design begins with listening. I take the time to understand your vision, needs, and lifestyle, ensuring every detail resonates with your personal story. My goal is to transform your space into a sanctuary where you can recharge, find serenity, and truly be yourself.

My Working Process

In every project, I aim to create a seamless experience, ensuring that every step reflects my commitment to quality and attention to detail. Here’s how I approach my work:

1

Concept

I begin with brainstorming and developing a clear vision for the project. Understanding your needs and preferences is essential for crafting a tailored design.

2

Prepare

Next, I gather materials and create initial sketches or plans. This step ensures we have everything needed before diving into the design process.

3

Retouch

I meticulously refine the design, making adjustments as necessary. This is where the vision starts to take shape and come to life.

4

Finalize

Finally, I present the completed design to you, ensuring that it aligns with your vision. Your feedback is invaluable in finalizing the project.

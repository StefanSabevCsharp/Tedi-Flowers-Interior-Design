Floral Design

I'm dedicated to helping you maximize the beauty and functionality of your living space.

I find flowers so fascinating. Their vibrant colors and diverse shapes captivate our senses, evoking emotions and enhancing our surroundings. They connect us to nature, reminding us of the joy and wonder found in the simplest things. Whether in celebration or comfort, flowers hold a special place in our hearts and lives.

1. Subscription
My exclusive floral subscription offers a curated selection of stunning blooms, designed to bring beauty and elegance to your home or office. The arrangements are crafted from the finest seasonal flowers, keeping your space fresh and exciting! Whether you want weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly deliveries, we’ve got you covered.

2. Seasonal Boutique
Perfect for gifting, special occasions, or simply treating yourself to everyday beauty.

3. Floral Design & Events
Make your special occasion unforgettable with our bespoke floral design services! From private parties to corporate events, we create exquisite arrangements that reflect your unique vision and style. I offer tailored designs that capture the essence of your event, using the freshest seasonal blooms. My passion for flowers and keen eye for design ensure that your event will be both elegant and memorable.

  • Thoughtfully curated floral arrangements for every occasion.
  • Unique designs that celebrate the beauty of nature.
  • Expertly crafted arrangements tailored to your style.
  • Fresh, seasonal flowers that brighten your space.

Interior Design

I’m here to help you unlock the full potential of your home. Transform your spaces into exquisite reflections of your style. Our interior design services combine aesthetics with functionality, ensuring that every corner of your home or office is both beautiful and practical.

Design Consultation/Concept/Furniture Selection/Colour schemes/Decor

I will take a close look at your current layout, furnishings, and architectural features to identify opportunities for improvement. My goal is to provide practical solutions that optimize both comfort and functionality.

We will begin by discussing your vision—your desired aesthetic, mood, and any specific requirements. Together, we’ll explore different design styles, materials, textures, and colors that resonate with you, ensuring a cohesive look that elevates your living experience.

Following our discussion, I’ll outline actionable steps and suggest furniture, lighting, and accessories that align with your vision. If needed, I can source all items for you and coordinate any technical support required.

Ultimately, my aim is for you to feel completely at home in your newly designed space. Let’s collaborate to create a distinctive living experience that reflects your style and enhances your quality of life! Contact me today to schedule your consultation.

  • Custom designs tailored to your lifestyle and preferences.
  • Comprehensive space planning to maximize functionality.
  • Access to exclusive furnishings and décor items.
  • Expert guidance on color schemes and materials.
